Meet Tellik

Who is Tellik?

Tellik is the mascot of Tellik

What is Tellik?

Tellik is an anthropomorphic raccoon-tanuki (as well as other raccoon-like creatures).

What is Tellik's gender?

Tellik is male

What are Tellik's specialties?

Tellik has the abilities of the Tanuki in Japanese folklore/mythology.

How big is Tellik?

Tellik is as tall as Rocket Raccoon from Guardians of the Galaxy (3 to 4 feet) tall (or around 120 cm tall). But in other circumstances he (and his relatives) are giants, reaching heights of tens, hundreds, or thousands of feet.

What does Tellik wear?

Tellik at times wears nothing, as he learned from his distant relative Foo. However, unlike Foo and the other characters, Tellik will wear just about anything.

What does Tellik like?

Tellik absolutely loves telling, reading, and watching stories.

What does Tellik dislike?

He dislikes unfair critics, as he sees value in all stories.

What is Tellik's personality?

Tellik is wise but also carefree.
